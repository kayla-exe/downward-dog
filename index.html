About Spirit Yoga

Our goal at Spirit Yoga is to provide you everything you need to begin your journey into mindfulness through the art of yoga. It is the core belief of everyone at Spirit Yoga that regular practice of yoga can improve your physical and mental health.

Yoga offers physical and mental health benefits for people of all ages, all body types and all ailments. Specific positions have been known to help different parts of the body, while also enabling a person to manage stress, slow heart rate and encourage mindfulness.

Take the first step in your journey to betterment with us at Spirit Yoga.

Why join us at Spirit Yoga?

Experienced Instructors

At Spirit Yoga, we take pride in teaching you yoga in a way that suits you. All our instructors have experience teaching, guiding and helping many different types of people through their journey through yoga. We are here to help you.

Equipment Provided

Here at Spirit Yoga, we understand that not everyone has the ability to get their hands on the necessary equipment for yoga classes. That's why we provide all equipment needed for your lesson on the day. All equipment is thoroughly sanitised after use.

1-to-1 Or Groups

Taking your first step on a new journey is nerve-wracking. We get it! At Spirit Yoga, we offer 1-to-1 classes with experienced instructors, but we also offer group classes if you want to bring your friend along too.

A Safe Space for All

For us at Spirit Yoga, it is important to us that all customers feel safe in our space. We have 0 tolerance towards offensive language or behaviour, and/or bigotry. We pride ourselves on being accepting of all types of peoples who want to begin their journey to betterment through yoga.
